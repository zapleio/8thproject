How it works

We make machine learning more accurate by collecting better data.

Most organizations have abundant data, but not the right data for accurate predictions. That’s often because the best decision makers don’t record their assumptions, and the worst performers hold back bad news. OnCorps addresses poor datasets by nudging people to collect better data and rewarding them with real time feedback.

How it works

Our personalized, high-return-on-use apps drive engagement.

Our Behavioral Science-based apps earn users’ buy-in and participation. Personalized nudges, targeted guidance and peer rankings give our apps a high return on use, which drives the positive loops that improve data collection and machine learning.

How it works

The system intelligently adapts to accelerate learning.

OnCorps adapts with each interaction to record better data, provide feedback and adjust recommendations. Personalized dashboards show performance rankings in real-time. As gaps are identified, leaders can make adjustments or introduce new apps and nudges.
